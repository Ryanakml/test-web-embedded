The UrbanStride Story

Founded in 2018 in Portland, Oregon, UrbanStride emerged from a simple observation: modern professionals deserved footwear that didn't compromise. We watched as people shuffled between dress shoes that pinched and sneakers that looked sloppy, sacrificing either comfort or credibility with each step.

Our founders, Maria Chen and David Kowalski, spent eighteen months traveling between artisan workshops in Italy, studying biomechanics labs in Germany, and collaborating with material scientists in Japan. The goal was singular: create footwear that performed as beautifully as it looked.

Our Philosophy

Every UrbanStride shoe begins with a question: What if we didn't have to choose? What if comfort and style, durability and elegance, tradition and innovation could coexist in every step?

We source materials from the world's finest tanneries—full-grain leathers from Tuscany, premium suedes from Portugal, and technical fabrics from Japanese mills. Each component is selected not just for its aesthetic appeal, but for its performance characteristics and environmental footprint.

Our construction methods blend centuries-old techniques with contemporary innovation. Goodyear welting ensures our boots can be resoled for years of wear. Memory foam insoles adapt to your unique gait. Water-resistant treatments protect against the elements without compromising breathability.

Today, UrbanStride serves professionals across 28 countries who refuse to accept mediocrity in any aspect of their lives. From boardrooms in Singapore to cobblestones in Stockholm, our shoes carry people who stride with purpose.

Sustainability Commitment

We believe great shoes should leave light footprints. Our vegetable-tanned leathers minimize chemical waste. Our packaging is 100% recyclable. And our construction methods prioritize longevity—every shoe we make is designed to be repaired, not replaced.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do UrbanStride shoes fit?

Our shoes are designed to fit true to size based on US sizing standards. We recommend ordering your standard dress shoe size for our oxfords, derbies, and loafers. For our sneakers and trainers, most customers prefer their athletic shoe size. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for leather styles (they'll mold to your foot) and down for sneakers. Each product page includes specific sizing notes based on that style's last.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free standard shipping on all US orders over $200. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for $25. International shipping is available to select countries, with rates calculated at checkout. All orders are processed within 1 business day and ship from our Portland facility. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 60-day return window for unworn shoes in original condition with all tags attached. Returns are free for US customers—simply use the prepaid return label included in your package. Shoes must show no signs of wear (we recommend trying them on carpeted surfaces only). Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes. Every UrbanStride shoe is covered by our Craftsmanship Guarantee. We warrant against defects in materials and workmanship for one year from purchase date. This covers issues like sole separation, stitching failures, or material defects under normal wear conditions. Normal wear and tear, damage from improper care, or modifications are not covered. To initiate a warranty claim, email photos and your order number to warranty@urbanstride.com.

Can I exchange my shoes for a different size?

Absolutely. We want you to love your UrbanStride shoes. If the size isn't quite right, initiate a return through your account and place a new order for your preferred size. To ensure you get your new size quickly, we recommend placing the new order first, then returning the original pair. If you need sizing assistance, our customer service team is available via email or chat to help you find your perfect fit.

How should I care for my shoes?

Proper care extends the life of your shoes significantly. For leather: Use a horsehair brush after each wear to remove dirt. Apply leather conditioner every 4-6 weeks. For suede: Brush with a suede brush and use a suede protector spray. For all shoes: Use cedar shoe trees when not wearing to maintain shape and absorb moisture. Avoid wearing the same pair consecutive days—leather needs 24 hours to dry between wears. Detailed care instructions are included with each purchase.

Are UrbanStride shoes waterproof?

Our shoes are water-resistant but not waterproof. Most styles feature water-resistant treatments that protect against light rain and splashes. Our desert boots and chelsea boots offer enhanced water resistance. However, we don't recommend submerging any of our shoes in water or wearing them in heavy rain without additional protection. If your shoes do get wet, stuff them with newspaper and let them dry naturally at room temperature—never use direct heat sources.

Do you offer corporate or bulk orders?

Yes, we work with companies seeking premium footwear for employee gifts, uniforms, or corporate events. Bulk orders of 10+ pairs qualify for volume pricing. We can also arrange custom sizing sessions and offer coordinated delivery. For corporate inquiries, please email corporate@urbanstride.com with your requirements, and our team will create a custom proposal.

Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping (United States)

We offer two shipping options for all US orders:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): Free on orders over $200, $12 on orders under $200
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): $25 flat rate

Order Processing

All orders are processed within one business day (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays). Orders placed after 2:00 PM PST will be processed the next business day. You will receive an email confirmation when your order is placed, and another email with tracking information once your order ships.

International Shipping

We currently ship to the following countries and regions:

  • Canada: 5-8 business days, $25 flat rate
  • United Kingdom & European Union: 7-12 business days, $35 flat rate
  • Australia & New Zealand: 10-15 business days, $45 flat rate
  • Japan & South Korea: 8-12 business days, $35 flat rate

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country. These charges are not included in our shipping rates and vary by destination.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website. If you don't receive tracking information within 2 business days of placing your order, please contact customer service.

Delivery Issues

If your package shows as delivered but you haven't received it, please check with neighbors and building management first. If you still can't locate your package after 48 hours, contact us at support@urbanstride.com. We'll work with the carrier to investigate and will replace your order if it's confirmed lost.

Address Changes

We can modify shipping addresses only if your order hasn't shipped yet. Contact us immediately if you need to change your delivery address. Once a package is in transit, we cannot redirect it—you'll need to work with the carrier directly or wait for delivery to the original address.

Holidays

During peak holiday seasons (November-December), please allow an additional 1-3 business days for processing and delivery. We recommend ordering early to ensure delivery before specific dates. Holiday shipping cutoff dates are posted on our website and social media channels.

Returns & Refund Policy

Our Return Promise

We want you to absolutely love your UrbanStride shoes. If they're not perfect for you, we offer a generous 60-day return window from the delivery date. Returns are completely free for US customers, and we make the process as simple as possible.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must meet these conditions:

  • Returned within 60 days of delivery
  • Unworn with no signs of use (scuffs, creasing, dirt, or wear on soles)
  • Original box and all packaging materials included
  • All tags still attached
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase

Helpful tip: Try shoes on carpeted surfaces only to preserve their pristine condition for returns.

How to Initiate a Return

Returning your shoes is straightforward:

  • Log into your account at urbanstride.com and navigate to your order history
  • Select the order containing the item you wish to return
  • Click "Initiate Return" and select your reason (optional but helpful for us)
  • Print the prepaid return label that appears on screen (also emailed to you)
  • Pack the shoes securely in the original box with all materials
  • Affix the return label and drop off at any USPS location

Don't have access to a printer? Contact our customer service team and we'll mail you a return label.

Refund Processing

Once we receive your return, our quality team inspects it within 2 business days. If everything looks good, we'll process your refund immediately. Refunds are issued to your original payment method and typically appear within 3-5 business days, depending on your bank or credit card company.

You'll receive an email confirmation when your return is received and again when your refund is processed.

Exchanges

While we don't offer direct exchanges, the process is simple: Return your original pair for a full refund and place a new order for the size or style you prefer. This ensures you get your new shoes as quickly as possible rather than waiting for the return to process first.

If you're unsure about sizing, our customer service team is available via email, chat, or phone to help you find your perfect fit before ordering.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a tracked shipping method. Once we receive and approve your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. Please note that we cannot refund original shipping charges for international orders, and any customs fees paid are non-refundable.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. Email photos of the defect to support@urbanstride.com along with your order number. We'll send a replacement immediately at no charge, or process a full refund if you prefer. You don't need to return defective items unless we request them for quality analysis.

Final Sale Items

Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged. These are clearly labeled at checkout. Final sale items are still covered under our one-year Craftsmanship Guarantee for defects.

Return Abuse Policy

While we trust our customers, we reserve the right to refuse returns or refunds if we detect fraudulent activity or excessive returns (e.g., shoes worn and returned, serial returns after extended use). Our goal is to protect the experience for our honest customers while maintaining our generous return policy.

  • Chat: Available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM PST
